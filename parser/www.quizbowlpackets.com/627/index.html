2014 Prison Bowl

Target level: High school

Season primarily used: 2013-2014

Prison Bowl VII is a regular difficulty set written and edited by members of the Hunter College High School Quiz Bowl team, Zach Pace and Matt Hill from the University at Buffalo, and Rohan Nag from Vanderbilt University. Marianna Zhang from Hunter served as head editor.

This set was ranked "satisfactory" by PACE.
For more information about the set, see the forum link on the right.

See more details about this set on the Quizbowl Resource Center Database
