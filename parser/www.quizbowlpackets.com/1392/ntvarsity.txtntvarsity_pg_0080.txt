Tossup 21: Math (Geometry) - Computational (30 Seconds) 
You want to find the total number of oranges in a square pyramid of oranges five levels high with
base length of five oranges. The number of oranges in each level is an integer, and the topof the 
pyramid has one orange, because it’s a square with side length of one orange. What is the total 
number of oranges in this square pyramid? 
Answer: 55
