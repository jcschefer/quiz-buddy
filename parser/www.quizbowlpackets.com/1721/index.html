2015 Prison Bowl

Target level: High school

Season primarily used: 2014-2015

Prison Bowl VIII is a regular difficulty set written and edited by members of the Hunter College High School Quiz Bowl team, the Virginia Commonwealth University Quiz Bowl team, Rohan Nag from Vanderbilt University, and Zihan Zheng from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Albert Tai and coach Christopher Chilton from Hunter served as head editors.

This set was ranked "satisfactory" by PACE.

For more information about the set, see the forum link on the right.

See more details about this set on the Quizbowl Resource Center Database
