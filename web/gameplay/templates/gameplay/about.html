About us

Purpose

Quizbowl, like any other activity, requires practice for improvement. However, one of two problems always arise. If you're alone, the problem is that reading a question doesn't provide the same experience as listening to a reader. If you're in a group, the problem is that someone always has to sit out in order to read questions.

This website was designed to provide both a way for individuals to practice on their own and a way for groups to practice together without anyone sitting out.

About the Authors

This project began as a senior project at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. Since then, it has been refactored and improved with the hopes of becoming more useful to the Quizbowl community. We hope you enjoy!
